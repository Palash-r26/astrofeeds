What are the 12 Zodiac Signs?

Astrologers believe that a person's character and destiny are determined by the Sun, Moon, and planets, which are in exact alignment with that person when they are born. People can relate to the characteristics of the zodiac, which can help them better understand their own and other people's personality traits.

All zodiac signs with their birth dates and key characteristics are described in detail below.

Aries

Dates: March 21 - April 19
Symbol: The Ram
Element: Fire
Ruling Planet: Mars
Personality Traits: Aries are known for their boldness, leadership abilities, and competitive nature. They have an adventurous spirit and love challenges.
Strengths: Courageous, passionate, and adventurous.
Weaknesses: Impulsive, impatient, and prone to aggression.
Compatibility: Aries is most compatible with fellow air signs Leo and Sagittarius. They also make a good pair with Libra and Taurus.

Taurus

Dates: April 20 - May 20
Symbol: The Bull
Element: Earth
Ruling Planet: Venus
Personality Traits: Individuals born under the sign of Taurus are dependable, practical, and grounded. They value security and stability and are well known for their tenacity and persistence.
Strengths: Loyal, patient, and hardworking.
Weaknesses: Stubborn, possessive, and materialistic.
Compatibility: Taurus gets along best with the other earth signs, Capricorn and Virgo. Scorpio is another sign that shares great compatibility with the Taureans.

Gemini

Dates: May 21 - June 20
Symbol: The Twins
Element: Air
Ruling Planet: Mercury
Personality Traits: Geminis are known for their curious, adaptable, and sociable nature. They are sharp-witted and like having thought-provoking discussions.
Strengths: Versatile, expressive, and witty.
Weaknesses: Superficial, restless, and indecisive.
Compatibility: Gemini shares the best compatibility with fellow air signs Libra and Aquarius. They are also compatible with the fire sign Sagittarius.

Cancer

Dates: June 21 - July 22
Symbol: The Crab
Element: Water
Ruling Planet: Moon
Personality Traits: Cancerians are nurturing, intuitive, and compassionate. They have strong emotional connections and place a high value on family and home life.
Strengths: Sensitive, empathetic, and protective.
Weaknesses: Moody, clingy, and overly sensitive.
Compatibility: Cancer is most compatible with fellow water signs Scorpio and Pisces. The earth signs Virgo and Taurus also make a good pair with Cancerians.

Leo

Dates: July 23 - August 22
Symbol: The Lion
Element: Fire
Ruling Planet: Sun
Personality Traits: Charming, confident, and generous. They enjoy being the center of attention and possess a magnetic personality.
Strengths: Creative, passionate, and loyal.
Weaknesses: Arrogant, domineering, and self-centered.
Compatibility: Best with Aries and Sagittarius; Aquarius also makes for a terrific match.

Virgo

Dates: August 23 - September 22
Symbol: The Maiden
Element: Earth
Ruling Planet: Mercury
Personality Traits: Practical, analytical, and detail-oriented; they strive for perfection.
Strengths: Organized, diligent, and reliable.
Weaknesses: Critical, worrisome, and overly cautious.
Compatibility: Best with Taurus and Capricorn; also a good match with Scorpio and Cancer.

Libra

Dates: September 23 - October 22
Symbol: The Scales
Element: Air
Ruling Planet: Venus
Personality Traits: Fair-minded, diplomatic, and charming; they value harmony and deep relationships.
Strengths: Romantic, balanced, and sociable.
Weaknesses: Indecisive, superficial, and conflict-avoidant.
Compatibility: Best with Gemini and Aquarius; also compatible with Sagittarius, Leo, and Aries.

Scorpio

Dates: October 23 - November 21
Symbol: The Scorpion
Element: Water
Ruling Planet: Pluto (Western) / Mars (Vedic)
Personality Traits: Passionate, intense, and determined; drawn to the deeper mysteries of life.
Strengths: Loyal, intuitive, and ambitious.
Weaknesses: Jealous, secretive, and resentful.
Compatibility: Best with Cancer and Pisces; Taurus, Capricorn, and Virgo are also compelling matches.

Sagittarius

Dates: November 22 - December 21
Symbol: The Archer
Element: Fire
Ruling Planet: Jupiter
Personality Traits: Adventurous, optimistic, and philosophical; they love freedom and exploring new ideas.
Strengths: Adventurous, open-minded, and optimistic.
Weaknesses: Impulsive, tactless, and restless.
Compatibility: Best with Aries and Leo; Aquarius and Libra also pair well.

Capricorn

Dates: December 22 - January 19
Symbol: The Goat
Element: Earth
Ruling Planet: Saturn
Personality Traits: Responsible, disciplined, and ambitious; they are masters of self-control and have a practical approach to life.
Strengths: Responsible, disciplined, and self-control.
Weaknesses: Unforgiving, condescending, and expecting the worst.
Compatibility: Best with Taurus and Virgo; Scorpio and Pisces also make good matches.

Aquarius

Dates: January 20 - February 18
Symbol: The Water Bearer
Element: Air
Ruling Planet: Uranus (Western) / Saturn (Vedic)
Personality Traits: Progressive, original, and independent; they are deep thinkers who love helping others.
Strengths: Progressive, original, and humanitarian.
Weaknesses: Runs from emotional expression, temperamental, and uncompromising.
Compatibility: Best with Gemini and Libra; Aries and Sagittarius are also great matches.

Pisces

Dates: February 19 - March 20
Symbol: The Fish
Element: Water
Ruling Planet: Neptune (Western) / Jupiter (Vedic)
Personality Traits: Compassionate, artistic, and intuitive; they are very friendly and often find themselves in the company of very different people.
Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, and intuitive.
Weaknesses: Fearful, overly trusting, and a desire to escape reality.
Compatibility: Best with Cancer and Scorpio; Taurus and Capricorn also pair well.
