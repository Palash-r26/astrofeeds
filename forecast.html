What Are the Important Planetary Shifts Occurring in 2025?

In 2025, a celestial dance of transits will unfold as it does every year. However, the astrological predictions for 2025 hint at three major planetary shifts that could potentially reshape our lives in significant ways.

This year, Jupiter will have three transits.

First, in May 2025, Jupiter will move from Taurus to Gemini.
The second transit will happen in October when Jupiter will move to Cancer.
The third transit will occur in December when Jupiter will become retrograde in Gemini.
The movements of Jupiter in 2025 hold immense potential to shape various aspects of our lives, as revealed by the 2025 horoscope.

As per the astrology forecast 2025, this year will also experience two more significant transits.

  • In addition, Rahu will be transiting from Pisces to Aquarius in May.
